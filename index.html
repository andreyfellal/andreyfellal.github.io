ℹ️About Me

Hey there, I’m Andrey, a bonafide native of Medianeira, a hidden gem nestled in the heart of southern Brazil. This charming little town has been my home sweet home for as long as I can remember. The world outside its borders has always held a magnetic pull, full of endless adventures waiting to be uncovered. Family is the cornerstone of my life. I’m blessed beyond measure with a stunning wife who’s my rock, and four incredible kids who keep our world spinning with laughter, chaos, and unconditional love. When I manage to snatch a moment for myself amidst the whirlwind of parenthood, I find solace in the comfort of my trusty Ford Galaxy, exploring the open road, or diving deep into the digital realm with my powerful computer. There’s something undeniably magical about the way life unfolds in Medianeira. From the warm, welcoming community to the breathtaking natural beauty that surrounds us, it’s a place where dreams take root and flourish. Thanks!
